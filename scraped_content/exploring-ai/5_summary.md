Throughout our journey, we’ve delved deep into the fundamental concepts and tools that Microsoft Azure offers in the field of Artificial Intelligence. We’ve explored the basic principles of AI, machine learning, computer vision, language processing, and generative AI, among others, and started building a strong foundation for AI development on Microsoft Azure.

It’s time to review the course contents and take a quick recap of what we’ve learned. Open the following boxes one by one and refresh your memory.

### Unit 1: Getting Started with Azure AI
</edukamu-collapse-hidden-title>

We started by familiarizing ourselves with the basic principles of artificial intelligence, exploring topics like machine learning, supervised learning, and unsupervised learning. We also took our first brief look at the AI capabilities offered in Microsoft Azure.

Consider the following questions. Can you find answers easily? You don’t need to return your answers.

* How would you describe artificial intelligence?
* What is machine learning?
* What is the difference between supervised and unsupervised learning?

### Unit 2: Azure and Computer Vision
</edukamu-collapse-hidden-title>

The second unit was all about computer vision, an exciting concept that enables intelligent devices to observe their surroundings. Facial recognition and character recognition were our main points of interest.

Consider the following questions. Can you find answers easily? You don’t need to return your answers.

* How would you describe computer vision?
* What are the main principles of facial recognition?
* How does character recognition differ from recognizing faces?

</edukamu-collapse>

### Unit 3: Azure and Natural Language Processing
</edukamu-collapse-hidden-title>

In the third unit, we shifted our focus to language. We reviewed the different ways in which AI works with language, exploring topics like natural language processing and conversational language understanding.

Consider the following questions. Can you find answers easily? You don’t need to return your answers.

* What are the main principles of natural language processing?
* What do AI applications need in order to recognize conversations?
* What are the main AI Speech features offered in Microsoft Azure?

</edukamu-collapse>

### Unit 4: Azure and Generative AI
</edukamu-collapse-hidden-title>

Generative AI has the potential to revolutionize the way we live and work, which made it a suitable topic for the fourth unit. We got to know how generative AI works while also getting familiar with copilots.

Consider the following questions. Can you find answers easily? You don’t need to return your answers.

* How does generative AI differ from “ordinary” AI?
* What are copilots? What do they do?
* How can we ensure responsible use of generative AI?

</edukamu-collapse>

### Unit 5: Azure and Document Intelligence
</edukamu-collapse-hidden-title>

In the fifth and final unit we focused on document intelligence. After studying the main aspects, we moved on to cognitive search and enriched data before wrapping up the course.

Consider the following questions. Can you find answers easily? You don’t need to return your answers.

* What are the core concepts of document intelligence?
* What can be accomplished with cognitive search?
* How would you describe enriched data? How does it differ from “regular” data?
