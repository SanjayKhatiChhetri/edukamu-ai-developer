### Unit 1: Foundations of Azure AI Services
</edukamu-collapse-hidden-title>

We started by familiarizing ourselves with Azure AI Services all the way from enabling them to using them in containers. We also took the first look at Azure AI Language.

Consider the following questions. Can you find answers easily? You don’t need to return your answers.

- What tools are included in Azure AI Services?
- How can using containers make Azure AI Services more powerful?
- How would you describe the main functions of Azure AI Language?

</edukamu-collapse>

### Unit 2: Natural Language Processing
</edukamu-collapse-hidden-title>

The second unit was all about natural language processing. While we’d also explored it during the previous courses, this time we took it a step further, covering topics like conversational language understanding and question answering.

Consider the following questions. Can you find answers easily? You don’t need to return your answers.

- What are the main capabilities and benefits of natural language processing?
- What are question answering and conversational language understanding? How do they differ?
- How do question answering and conversational language understanding complement each other?

</edukamu-collapse>

<edukamu-collapse isCollapsed="true" title-level="4">
<edukamu-collapse-hidden-title collapseType="aineisto">
### Unit 3: Retrieval Augmented Generation
</edukamu-collapse-hidden-title>

In the third unit we got acquainted with a topic that’s bound to become more and more important as time goes on: retrieval augmented generation. We got to know its basic principles and explored Azure AI Search, Microsoft’s cutting-edge solution for all RAG needs.

Consider the following questions. Can you find answers easily? You don’t need to return your answers.

- What is retrieval augmented generation?
- How is RAG implemented in Azure AI Search?
- In what ways can RAG improve NLP services?

</edukamu-collapse>

<edukamu-collapse isCollapsed="true" title-level="4">
<edukamu-collapse-hidden-title collapseType="aineisto">
### Unit 4: Implementing Generative AI
</edukamu-collapse-hidden-title>

In the final unit, we moved our focus to Azure OpenAI Services and generative AI. We explored the service’s main capabilities as well as prompt engineering and finished strong by reviewing some best practices for responsible AI usage.

Consider the following questions. Can you find answers easily? You don’t need to return your answers.

- How can you fine-tune prompts to make them as effective as possible?
- In what ways can generative AI make you a better programmer?
- What are the four stages of responsible AI development?

</edukamu-collapse>
