### Unit 1: Introduction to Azure
</edukamu-collapse-hidden-title>

We started off by taking a tour around Azure. We examined its basic characteristics, infrastructure, and computing services without forgetting the VMs. See if you can answer the following questions. You don’t need to return your answers.

* Can you describe the infrastructure of Azure?

### Unit 2: Data, Storage, and Safety
</edukamu-collapse-hidden-title>

The second unit was all about Azure services and safety. We began by looking at different storage options before moving on to data migration and authorization. Conditional access was also on the list. See if you can answer the following questions. You don’t need to return your answers.

* What services does Azure Storage offer?
* How does Azure keep your data safe?
* What is the meaning of conditional access?

</edukamu-collapse-hidden-title>
